About Me

Orjime Benjamin is a driven and passionate individual from Nigeria, specifically from Benue State, Makurdi Local Government Area. Born in July 1987, he is currently based in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) Abuja, Nigeria. As a graduate with a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry, Orjime has built a diverse and robust professional profile over the years. He possesses a wealth of experience spanning multiple industries, including manufacturing, education, technical, and analytical fields. His technological know-how and a keen interest in handwork further solidify his versatile skill set.

As a man of faith, Orjime is deeply rooted in his Christian beliefs and loves serving in God's vineyard. His faith plays a significant role in his life, guiding both his personal and professional pursuits. He believes in God as the CEO of his entrepreneurial venture, Universh Limited, where he holds the position of Managing Director. This company reflects his dedication to empowering others and making a meaningful impact on society.
